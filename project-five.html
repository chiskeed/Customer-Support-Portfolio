Resolving Password Reset Failure (Email Not Received)

Situation

A user was locked out of their Slack account after forgetting their password and was unable to receive the password reset email.

Despite multiple attempts and checking their spam folder, the email did not arrive. The issue was urgent, as the user needed immediate access to continue their work.

Task

My responsibility was to:

  • Restore the user’s access to their account as quickly as possible
  • Identify and address potential causes of the missing reset email
  • Prevent future login disruptions by taking ownership of the root issue
  • Deliver a smooth, reassuring support experience under urgency

Action

I approached the issue using a “stabilize first, diagnose second” framework:

  • Clarified urgency and goal
    • Confirmed the user’s priority was immediate access (not necessarily password change)
  • Provided an immediate workaround to restore access
    • Guided the user to log in using Google authentication (SSO)
    • This bypassed the password requirement and restored access instantly
  • Ensured continuity and user reassurance:
    • Confirmed successful login and reduced urgency pressure
  • Took ownership of the root cause (post-resolution)
    • Educated the user on possible reasons for the missing email
    • Set clear expectations for follow-up troubleshooting to prevent recurrence

Result

  • User regained account access within minutes
  • Urgent workflow disruption was eliminated
  • Frustration reduced through clear and empathetic communication
  • Potential repeat issue mitigated through proactive guidance
